# Tableau Sales Dashboard - April 2019

## Objective

Build a clean, executive-level Tableau dashboard to communicate sales performance and revenue drivers using a single transactional dataset.

## Dataset
April 2019 sales data containing order-level transactions, including product, city, quantity ordered, price, and revenue.

The same cleaned dataset used in the Python analysis (Weeks 5â€“6) was reused here to focus purely on visualization and communication.

## Dashboard Components

- **Daily Revenue Trend** - highlights revenue volatility and peak sales days

- **Revenue by City** - shows geographic concentration of revenue

- **Revenue by Product** - reveals revenue dependence on high-value products

- **City Filter** - enables quick comparison across locations

## Key Insights

- Revenue is not evenly distributed across the month, with clear daily peaks and dips

- A small number of cities contribute a disproportionate share of total revenue

- High-revenue products drive overall performance more than high-volume products

## Business Use Case

This dashboard is designed for executives and stakeholders who need a fast, high-level view of sales performance to support decisions around location strategy, inventory focus, and revenue monitoring.
